# Authors

Pocono Pet Medication Index is written as a pharmacy-supported reference project from the Pocono Community Pharmacy team.

## Approved author signatures

### Olivia Barkley, PharmD

Used for clinical pharmacy safety notes, species and weight reminders, NSAID questions, steroid notes, insulin notes, and pages where careful medication-safety framing is important.

Style note: careful, clinical, and practical. A recurring principle is that the medication name is only one part of the question.

### Roger Hay, Pharmacist

Used for pharmacy counter conversations, refill checklists, label questions, travel planning, and owner preparation pages.

Style note: practical and direct. A recurring principle is to have the bottle nearby before calling.

### Robert Smith, Pharmacist

Used for medication access, refill workflow, compounded medication questions, antibiotics, chronic medication continuity, and pharmacy-veterinary handoff notes.

Style note: workflow-centered and communication-focused. A recurring principle is that a clear label protects the pet, the owner, and the prescriber’s intent.

### Pocono Community Pharmacy Team

Used for general project pages, repository orientation, and shared reference notes.

## Scope of authorship

These authors are presented as pharmacy professionals and pharmacy team contributors. They are not presented as veterinarians, veterinary doctors, or veterinary prescribers in this project.

## Source context

The project is designed as a companion-style reference connected to [Pocono Community Pharmacy](https://www.poconocommunitypharmacy.com/) with [Pocono pet medication support](https://www.poconocommunitypharmacy.com/medication-information/pet-medication-support) as the primary medication-support source and the [Pet Care service page](https://www.poconocommunitypharmacy.com/pet-care) as a broader service/support page.
