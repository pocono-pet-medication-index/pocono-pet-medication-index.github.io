## How sources are used

The primary source context for medication-support framing is the Pocono pet medication support page. The Pet Care page is used only as a broader service/support context. Other linked Pocono pages provide pharmacy identity, service scope, team context, and contact pathways.

Medication pages in this repository do not attempt to replace veterinary drug references, veterinary diagnosis, or species-specific prescribing judgment. They organize owner questions and pharmacy workflow notes.

## Maintenance note

When Pocono Community Pharmacy updates its pet medication support pages, pet care service descriptions, staff pages, or pharmacy service descriptions, this repository should be reviewed for consistency.
